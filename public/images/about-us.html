South American Initiative is a U.S. based non-profit organization working to help orphanages, hospitals, abandoned pets and zoo animals in South America who are starving with no food, no medicine, no clean drinking water, and no medical supplies.

Venezuela Needs Help Right Now

immediate focus for 2019-2020 is to provide rapid help for the people of Venezuela who cannot feed themselves or get basic life-saving medicines because the current political and economic crisis is causing life-threatening hardships for the most vulnerable.

Campaigns & Projects

More projects are scheduled to be added to continue to help those who cannot provide the basics they need to survive.

During the first months of the year 2018, the list of people helped increased as the number of people in need continues to explode. Now, there are more than 23,000 people benefiting from the food programs. SAI has provided more than 70,000 meals for Venezuelan orphans, hospital patients and families living in total despair.

Malnutrition #1 Cause of Illness & Death for Infants, Hospital Patients, Abandoned Pets & Zoo Animals

SAI provides food, medicine, and medical supplies to orphanages where parents have been forced to abandon their children because they can no longer afford to feed them. Malnutrition is the number one cause of illness and death for infants, hospital patients, family pets, and zoo animals.

Shelters for Abandoned Pets & Zoo Refuges

In addition to helping people who live in Venezuela, SAI has also focused on stray animals and zoo animals to guarantee their survival and well being. That's why the Venezuela Zoo Animal Rescue Program and Help Abandoned Pets campaigns have become an important project for this generous cause.

How SAI Delivers Safe & Easy Donations To Help Venezuelans

SAI is a U.S. based non-profit, Non Government Organization supported by private citizens and large Fortune 500 corporations in America who see the value and need in helping Venezuelans overcome their current chaos and economic crisis.

Second Only To The Red Cross In Venezuela

SAI is second only to the Red Cross when it comes to providing food, medicine, and emergency care for the most vulnerable people in Venezuela. In fact, SAI has received international awards for their advanced technology, transparency, and significant impact it has created in a short time with limited donations.

SAI also maintains a network of backup generators to provide electricity during even the worst conditions. When everyone else is experiencing a blackout, SAI facilities remain operational with reliable power generators.

Our Values

Integrity

When people need help and their lives are in danger, they turn to those they can trust. SAI operates with integrity to make sure donors can give with certainty and those in need get the food, and medical care they need to survive.

Accountability

SAI is supported by proprietary technology that not only provides a high level of transparency and accountability, but also thrives off of keeping our word and commitments because we know individual lives depend on what we do.

Respect

At SAI we know respect is a two way street. To get it, you’ve got to also give it. We know that every life counts and without the essentials like food, water, shelter, clothing, and education, it becomes increasingly difficult to build a good life and a strong family.

Creativity

SAI utilizes proprietary technology to accomplish difficult tasks that would be impossible without an army of volunteers and staff. With state of the art communications and high-tech software, SAI is able to accomplish far more and help more people in need than ever before.
